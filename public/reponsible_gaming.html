General Betting Rules
All the provisions established for Pre-Game Betting, where possible and unless otherwise stated, apply also to In Play betting. With the exception of In Play betting, Pre-Game bets placed after the event start time will be considered void. In the case of combo bets, only the selection involved will be voided. In such circumstances, the combo bet remains valid.
Additional or new markets may be made available by tether.bet from time to time. It is the responsibility of the Customer to read the “Market Rules” applicable before placing any bets. In the event of any dispute, the Customer agrees that tether.bet’s records shall determine the outcome of any claim.
Identity and Age Verification:
Gambling under the age of 18 is not permitted. The company will carry out identity and age verification checks upon registration. Accounts failing age verification will be closed.
Who is not allowed to bet:
Bets on events in which the person betting is participating, or those which are commissioned to others by any person participating, are not permitted.
Liability/Data Processing:
The company does not assume liability for data input or transmission errors, this includes any form of result messaging, for example, SMS. The company also assumes no liability for the accuracy of live scores, statistics, and intermediate results via live betting. The company further reserves the right to void bets if any such bet was won by the bettor as a result of a technical fault or error, provided the company is able to forensically prove the technical fault or error. In all such circumstances, the bet will be declared void and stakes returned to the bettor account. The company reserves the right to hold the customer liable for any damage caused to the company as a result of such user knowingly taking advantage of a technical or administrative error when making and/or receiving payments.
Past Posting:
Placing a bet on an already known outcome is not permitted. In such circumstances bets accepted in error will be declared void. If the bettor is seen to persistently place bets on already known outcomes, the company reserves the right to cease serving said bettor including the confiscation of their account balance.
Palpable Errors:
The company reserves its right to correct obvious errors, before and after the event. Bets inadvertently accepted in such circumstances will be declared void and the stake returned.
Collusion:
Customers must register as unique individuals. If the company believes, before or after the official outcome of the selections is known, that customers are acting in collusion or as a syndicate for the purpose of manipulating betting markets, it reserves the right to void bets and withhold payment of any kind, and confiscate the customer account balance.
Fraud and Collusion:
tether.bet is for sole use only and is for personal entertainment only. tether.bet allows only one account per person. Any subsequent accounts opened under the same postcode/personal details/IP address or syndication which is found to be related to any existing account will be closed immediately and any bets will be voided at tether.bet’s discretion. tether.bet reserves the right to withhold all or part of the balance and/or recover from the account deposits, payouts, bonuses, any winnings which is attained by these means
Match Fixing or Price Rigging:
In the event that the company suspects any event manipulation, it withholds the right, in its absolute discretion, to withdraw or suspend any or all betting opportunities, within the said event and the company may withhold payment on any such event or market until the respective sports federation has determined its investigation. Furthermore, if the company believes that match or price rigging has taken place, it reserves the right to share your personal data to the respective authorities or any other third party, as part of these investigations.
Stakes
There must be sufficient funds on the account to cover the betting stake(s). Bets will be accepted only in the order in which they are received and fully covered.
Bet Placement
A bet is accepted once the company has confirmed it. Bets will appear in the customer account section “My Bets”. Bets which do not directly appear on tether.bet’s files or records are not valid. If you are unsure about the validity of a bet, please check your Account or contact our Customer Service. It is up to the Customer to verify that the number of bets placed, and the amount staked are correct. All odds are subject to possible changes. The official odds are those indicated by tether.bet in the bet receipt.
Void Bets:
If a bet is declared “invalid” or “void”, for individual bets (singles) your stake is returned to your account balance. Combo or system bets, the void bet will be evaluated as won with odds of 1.00 for the void bet(s) only, and the total odds for the combo or system bet will be recalculated.
Accepted Bets:
If the bet – for whatever reasons – is placed after the beginning of the betting event, the bet is void, this does not include ‘in play’ betting.
Cancellation
tether.bet has the right to refuse, accept, or partially accept any bet. Furthermore, tether.bet has the right to cancel all accepted bets before the betting event begins without any explanation. In such cases, a communication informing the customer of the cancellation of the bet will be sent to the customer registered email address.
Payout:
The Customer has an obligation to inform tether.bet as soon as possible of any amount wrongly credited on his/her account. tether.bet may at any time offset any positive balance resulting from the erroneous operation, as well as cancel possible bets or transactions made with the erroneously credited funds.
Winning limits:
Unlimited
Sport Rules
Sport specific rules will always supersede General Betting Rules
All bets stand:
Unless otherwise stated, the principle “all bets stand” applies to all bets. This means, that if the athlete or team, etc., on whom/which the bet has been placed, fails to participate – for whatever reason – the bet is lost, unless, but not limited to:

● The event or tournament is canceled or declared void
● The venue has changed
● Live betting (Non-Runner = No bet)
● Head to Head betting = If one or more participant is withdrawn
● The event or tournament is interrupted for a period of more than 24 hours after the original start date
Determined bets:
For all won bets, where the outcome is already decided, are settled as won, regardless of subsequent abandonment of the event/tournament at any time.
Postponement 24 hours rule
The company recognizes only those results, which have been achieved on the playing field or platform. Results amounting from disciplinary measures from a sports court or another court of law are not considered. In case of violation of sports laws, the company reserves the right to freeze the winnings and withhold payment. If any selection does not participate in an event (except the “All bets stand”) principle, or if one of the selected events is abandoned or canceled or does not take place for other reasons, and the respective betting events are not held within 24 hours, these bets are evaluated as “void” and original stake will be refunded..
Related Bets:
Related bets are two or more bets that have related contingency. Any related contingency bet(s) inadvertently accepted will be determined void and the betting stakes returned.
Dead Heats (tie):
If there is more than one athlete or team, or horse achieving the same placing, the following formula will apply Original Odds / ‘X’ (if 2 winners, X = 2, if 3 winners, X = 3, etc.)
Playing Times:
In all sports, only regular playing time counts unless otherwise stated (including injury/added time) Any extra time or penalty shoot-outs, etc. will have no influence on the settlement of any regular time bets.
Disqualifications:
For all bets referring to the final result of the event/tournament, any player/starter/team that is disqualified for whatever reasons within 24 hours after the end of the event/tournament will be deemed a loser.
Odd or Even betting:
If the result is “0” then bets will be settled as “Even”.
Handicap/Spread:
The event must be completed for bets to stand.
Totals Betting (bets containing the betting options ‘Over’ and ‘Under’):
If the event is abandoned before its completion then all bets will be void, unless the highest possible total outcome has already been achieved, in which case all bets stand.
Head-to-Head (H2H) betting:
All selections must take part, or bets will be void
Medal betting:
All bets on the number of medals will be settled on the official medal table at the end of the event (e.g. Olympic Games, World Championships, …). Any changes made by any governing body at a later date do not count for betting purposes. Team medals: Any medals won by a team/nation per competition count as one medal regardless of the number of team members.
